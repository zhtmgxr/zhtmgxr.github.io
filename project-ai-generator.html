Project summary

    Artify serves as a web application to support image generation based on Artificial Intelligence(AI). The product adopts several most popular and representative Stable Diffusion 1.5 and Stable Diffusion XL models that are deployed locally on the server client’s GPU and driven via the AUTOMATIC1111/stable-diffusion-webui to create images from user-inputted text prompts and parameters. Furthermore, Artify allows users to use the pre-trained ESRGAN model that is directly deployed locally on the server client’s GPU to upscale their uploaded images. The resulting operations are run at the cost of the user’s credits.

    Artify provides a bridge between AI technology and layman users, and offers an interface to profes- sionals like artists, creators, internet celebrities, and general users who are interested in the technology and would like to boost their imagination. This product may make the AI technique more accessible to users who do not have much knowledge of engineering, AI, and programming.
