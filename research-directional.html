Directional dependence statistic based on Chatterjee’s correlation

Research assistant · The Chinese University of Hong Kong · Supervisor: Prof. Keith Chan

Previous Next

Project summary

  • Studied the directional statistic Δq = q(X,Y) − q(Y,X) constructed from Chatterjee’s rank correlation.
  • Established consistency and key invariance properties under mild dependence settings.
  • Derived asymptotic distribution results (LLN and CLT) and discussed implications for inference.
